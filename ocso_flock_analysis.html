Camera Deployment Timeline

Understanding the Purchase Orders:

The confusion in the purchase orders comes from them showing both new camera purchases and annual subscription renewals for existing cameras. The quantities listed don't all represent new cameras being added.

Actual Camera Additions:

  • September 2021: 75 cameras (initial deployment)
  • November 2022: 2 Falcon cameras added
  • January 2023: 1 camera at airport
  • November 2023: 6 new cameras at airport
  • December 2023: 20 cameras added

Subscription Renewals (not new cameras):

  • October 2024: Annual renewal for 53 cameras (Year 2)
  • October 2025: Annual renewal for 53 cameras (Year 3 of 60-month term)
Note: The discrepancy between the total cameras mentioned in various POs (which could suggest 104+ cameras if all were cumulative) and the official count of 79 cameras likely reflects cameras being relocated, replaced, or decommissioned over time.
