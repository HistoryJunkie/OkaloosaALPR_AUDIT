# Florida Statute 316.0778
## Automated License Plate Recognition Systems; Records Retention

**Source:** [Florida Statutes - Section 316.0778](https://www.leg.state.fl.us/Statutes/index.cfm?App_mode=Display_Statute&Search_String=&URL=0300-0399/0316/Sections/0316.0778.html)

---

## (1) Definition

As used in this section, the term "automated license plate recognition system" means a system of one or more mobile or fixed high-speed cameras combined with computer algorithms to convert images of license plates into computer-readable data.

---

## (2) Retention Schedule Requirement

In consultation with the Department of Law Enforcement, the Department of State shall establish a retention schedule for records containing images and data generated through the use of an automated license plate recognition system. The retention schedule must establish a maximum period that the records may be retained.
