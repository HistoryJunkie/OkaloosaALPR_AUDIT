Dear Public Records Officer:

Pursuant to Florida's Public Records Law, Chapter 119, Florida Statutes, I am requesting the following records related to policies, procedures, and training for the Okaloosa County Sheriff's Office ALPR (Automated License Plate Recognition) system:

### Policy Documents

1. **ALPR Use Policy** - Any written policy governing:
   - Authorized uses of the ALPR system
   - Prohibited uses
   - Search requirements and restrictions
   - Justification/documentation requirements
   - Supervisor approval requirements
   - Data retention and deletion procedures

2. **Data Sharing Policy** - Any policy governing:
   - Sharing ALPR data with other agencies
   - Requirements for external agency access
   - Restrictions on data sharing
   - Audit or logging of shared access

3. **Hot List Management Policy** - Any policy governing:
   - Criteria for adding plates to watch lists
   - Approval process for manual additions
   - Review and expiration procedures
   - Categories of permitted alerts

4. **Privacy Policy** - Any policy addressing:
   - Citizen privacy protections
   - Data minimization practices
   - Public disclosure procedures
   - Complaint handling

5. **Audit Policy** - Any policy governing:
   - Review of ALPR searches
   - Identification of misuse
   - Disciplinary procedures
   - Reporting requirements

### Training Materials

6. **ALPR Training Curriculum** - All materials used to train deputies on:
   - System operation
   - Policy compliance
   - Legal requirements
   - Privacy obligations
   - Proper documentation

7. **Training Records** - Documentation of:
   - Which personnel have received ALPR training
   - Training completion dates
   - Certification or qualification requirements

8. **Flock Safety Training Materials** - Any training materials provided by Flock Safety, including:
   - User guides
   - Best practices documents
   - Compliance guidance

### Oversight Documents

9. **Audit Reports** - Any internal or external audits of ALPR use, including:
   - Compliance reviews
   - Use pattern analysis
   - Identified violations or concerns
   - Corrective actions taken

10. **Complaints and Investigations** - Records of any:
    - Citizen complaints about ALPR use
    - Internal investigations of ALPR misuse
    - Disciplinary actions related to ALPR

### Preferred Format

Please provide records in electronic format (PDF preferred) to: [email address]

### Response Timeline

Per Florida Statutes Section 119.07, I expect a response within a reasonable time.

Thank you for your assistance.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]
[Contact Information]

## Notes for Requestor

- Policy documents are generally public records
- Training materials may have some redactions for security techniques
- Discipline records may be partially exempt
- Internal audits should be largely disclosable
