Dear Public Records Officer:

Pursuant to Florida's Public Records Law, Chapter 119, Florida Statutes, I am requesting copies of the following records related to the Okaloosa County Sheriff's Office contract with Flock Safety (Flock Group, Inc.):

### Contract Documents

1. **Order Form (Exhibit A)** to the Master Services Agreement dated September 22, 2023, including:
   - Pricing and fee schedules
   - Number and type of devices
   - Service tiers and features purchased
   - Billing terms and payment schedules

2. **Insurance Certificate (Exhibit B)** referenced in MSA Section 8.5, including:
   - Coverage types and limits
   - Policy numbers
   - Expiration dates

3. **Customer Implementation Guide (Exhibit C)** referenced in MSA Section 10.4

4. **Any amendments, addenda, or modifications** to the Master Services Agreement since September 22, 2023

5. **Any special terms** agreed to pursuant to MSA Section 11.7

6. **All purchase orders** issued to Flock Safety since contract inception

7. **All invoices** received from Flock Safety and corresponding payment records

8. **Any correspondence** between OCSO and Flock Safety regarding:
   - Contract modifications
   - Service issues or disputes
   - Data retention policies
   - System capabilities or features
