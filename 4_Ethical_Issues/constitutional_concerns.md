## 2. Mosaic Theory Analysis

### Concept
Individual data points (single plate reads) may be constitutionally insignificant, but aggregation creates a "mosaic" revealing protected information.

### ALPR Mosaic Capabilities

| Individual Read | Aggregated Pattern | Privacy Impact |
|-----------------|-------------------|----------------|
| Vehicle at location X | Regular visits to location | Reveals associations |
| Time of travel | Daily routine mapping | Reveals lifestyle |
| Route taken | Comprehensive movement history | Reveals private activities |

### Evidence from OCSO Audit

- **524-day lookback:** Can reconstruct 1.5 years of movements
- **6,709 networks:** Nationwide tracking capability
- **100,034 devices:** Comprehensive geographic coverage
- **46.83% multi-network:** Nearly half of searches go beyond local

## 4. Procedural Due Process

### Concerns

1. **No Pre-Search Judicial Review**
   - Officers query system without warrant
   - No probable cause requirement documented
   - "Reason" field often generic or blank

2. **No Post-Search Accountability**
   - Officer names redacted
   - Case numbers redacted
   - No way to challenge improper searches

3. **Lack of Notice**
   - Citizens unaware they are being tracked
   - No notification when searched
   - No mechanism to challenge inclusion
