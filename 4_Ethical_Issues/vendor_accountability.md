## 2. Marketing Claims vs. Reality

### Claim: "30-Day Rolling Deletion"

**Flock/OCSO Claim:**
> "Flock Safety deletes its data every 30 days on a rolling basis" (OCSO FAQ)

**Reality:**
- 463 searches accessed data older than 30 days
- Maximum lookback: 524 days (1.5 years)
- Routine searches (patrol, traffic) accessed year-old data

**Assessment:** MISLEADING
