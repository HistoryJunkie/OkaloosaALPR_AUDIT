### Policy Recommendations

1. **Define Permitted Uses**
   - Enumerate specific authorized purposes
   - Prohibit general patrol surveillance
   - Require case number for all searches

2. **Limit Network Scope**
   - Require justification for multi-network searches
   - Supervisor approval for >X networks
   - Prohibit nationwide dragnets without warrant

3. **Enforce Retention Limits**
   - Technical controls on historical access
   - Exception process for legitimate investigations
   - Audit of all >30-day searches

4. **Transparency Requirements**
   - Public reporting on use categories
   - Annual disclosure of search statistics
   - Camera location disclosure
