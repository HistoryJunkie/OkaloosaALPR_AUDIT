## **Footage vs Customer Data Relationship**

- Customer Data = "data, media and content provided by Customer through the Services. **For the avoidance of doubt, the Customer Data will include the Footage.**"
- Footage = "still images, video, audio and other data captured by the Flock Hardware or Customer Hardware" (MSA ยง1.10)

## **30-Day Retention Policy**

**What gets deleted after 30 days:**
- **Footage** (actual camera captures) - deleted on rolling 30-day basis (MSA Recitals)
- Unless designated as evidence or otherwise specified in Order Form

**What's retained indefinitely:**
- **User queries with user profiles** - retained indefinitely for auditing (FAQ)
- **Evidence** - any footage designated as evidence stored in Evidence.Com indefinitely (FAQ)
- **Audit logs** - all requests and queries are logged and auditable (FAQ)

## **Critical Nuances**

**Hot Plates:**
- 30-day expiration by default
- Administrator reviews after expiration to determine if extension needed
- Removed once investigative purpose achieved

**Customer responsibilities:**
- Must extract, download, and archive footage to own storage devices (MSA Recitals)
- If agency wants footage beyond 30 days without evidence designation, they must download it themselves

**Data ownership:**
- Customer retains all ownership of Customer Data (including Footage) (MSA ยง4.1)
- Flock receives limited license to use for providing services
- Flock does not own and shall not sell Customer Data

**Disclosure rights:**
- During Retention Period, Flock may disclose Footage to law enforcement/government if legally required or good faith belief it's necessary for legal process, security, fraud, technical issues, or emergencies (MSA ยง5.3)
